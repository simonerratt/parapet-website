Parapet Solutions has been created to provide quality IT services to businesses of all sizes throughout Australia.

We have concentrated on solutions that remove complexity and lower the administrative overhead for our customers:

  • Microsoft for cloud computing and virtualisation
  • Huawei hardware for on-premises computing
  • Wyse vWorkspace software for VDI
